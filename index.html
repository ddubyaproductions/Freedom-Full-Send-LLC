About Freedom Full Send

Based in New Braunfels, Texas, and led by Darrell Williams, Freedom Full Send is a non-profit organization dedicated to giving back to our nation's heroes. We host monthly drone fly days in San Antonio and surrounding communities.

As featured in DJI's "On the Road" documentary series, our mission demonstrates how drone technology helps veterans heal invisible wounds like PTSD. Beyond the technology, our events create moments of relaxation, decompression, and mutual support.

Our Core Pillars

Veteran Camaraderie

A safe, welcoming place to chat, laugh, and connect with fellow service members who understand your journey.

Hands-on Flying

Inclusive events welcoming everyone from first-time drone pilots to seasoned FPV flyers.

Mental Health

Harnessing the calm, focus, and freedom of flight to help veterans decompress and find peace.
