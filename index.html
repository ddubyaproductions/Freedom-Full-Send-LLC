About Our Mission

At Freedom Full Send, we are dedicated to providing a welcoming space where Veterans can connect, share stories, and build camaraderie. Based out of New Braunfels, we host monthly drone fly days for Veterans in San Antonio and the surrounding communities.

Whether you are a first-time pilot or a seasoned flyer, our events offer hands-on drone flying designed to provide profound mental health benefits. The calm, focus, and freedom of flight create moments of relaxation, decompression, and mutual support.

Register for an Event
