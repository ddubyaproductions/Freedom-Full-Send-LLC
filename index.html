Our Mission

We host monthly drone fly days dedicated to giving back to our nation's heroes. These gatherings provide a welcoming space where Veterans can connect, share stories, and build camaraderie while discovering the therapeutic benefits of flying drones.

Each session offers:

  • Veteran Camaraderie: A place to chat, laugh, and connect in a supportive environment.
  • Hands-On Flying: Perfect for first-time pilots and seasoned flyers alike.
  • Mental Health Benefits: Experiencing the calm, focus, and absolute freedom of flight.
  • Community Connections: Building lasting bonds that extend well beyond the event.
